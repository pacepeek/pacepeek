More about the features

Repository Tracking

Track your progress in real-time. Free users can track public repositories, while Premium users get access to both public and private repository tracking.

Custom Widgets

Premium users can create and customize embeddable widgets to showcase their progress on their portfolio, blog, or website.

X Integration

Connect your X (Twitter) account to automatically share your progress also on X. Your posts will be shared to X in real-time automatically.

10 posts per day

Every user gets up to 10 posts per day. Posts are made when there is enough progress. This is done to keep the leaderboard fair.
